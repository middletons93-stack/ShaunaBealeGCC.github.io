Welcome to Lochquarry Outdoor Centre!


We are a small team dedicated to helping adventurous adults and young people to find adventure in a safe and controlled environment.
We offer a range of activities for various group sizes, suitable from age 6+.

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills.
On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery, but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities.
With activities designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people, Lochquarry truly brings adventure to everyone!

We are happy to accommodate bookings from Uniformed Youth Groups, Schools and Individual or Private groups!
We also welcome corporate bookings for team building and events!
For enquiries or to make a booking, please call us directly on 01475 229 8311.
